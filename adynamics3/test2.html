  4. Foreign License Conversion:

    Get your Foreign license converted to DGCA CPL by completing 5 Checks

    120 Nautical Miles Cross Country Flying at Night
    250 Nautical Miles Cross Country Flying in the Day
    General Flight Test within Airport Emergency both at Nighttime and Daytime
    Instrument Rating Check
    15 Hours Flying time in the last 6 months
